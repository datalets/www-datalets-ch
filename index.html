What is data?

Datalets is the development practice of Oleg Lavrovsky based in Berne, Switzerland, well grounded in data architecture, agile engineering and beautiful code, ready to accelerate projects in any domain with open data and open systems, research insights and communicate findings with visualization, and to help solve human problems with accessible and usable technology. Please be in touch if you have any questions or would like us to work together.
